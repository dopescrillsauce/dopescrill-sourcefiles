Who is Dan Meier? Instead of just jotting down the typical cliche' self-endorsements, I will just tell you that I love working on new and exciting projects that WE can sit back and say "Wow...that was a lot of fun and the end result is truly great." I come from an urban planning & design background that exposed me to the digital world all while contributing to my realization for my passion in the digital design and computer sciences.

I love the process of finding the core problems, concerns, or desires and then designing the solution. I'm not into taking life more seriously then it has too be, and genuinely want to do great work and have fun doing it. I've been lucky enough to study, travel, and work in exciting places around the world, and am excited to travel to many more places in the future (my bucket list is to visit every Continent). I look forward to getting in touch and doing some great work together.
